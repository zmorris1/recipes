Ingredients:
-3 eggs
-1 1/4 cups milk
-3/4 cups flour
-1 tablespoon sugar
-1 teaspoon salt
-1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
1.  Mix the milk and eggs with an egg beater.  Mix in the dry ingredients as you go until the batter has a smooth texture.

2.  Melt a lot of butter on a large, flat, round frying pan.

3.  Scoop 1 medium measuring cup of batter and pour into a circle on the pan.

4.  When pancake solidifies, flip with a spatula.  Cook until golden brown.

Serves 2-3.
